Brief Description

The client requested an attractive darker website, wich would echo the idea of guiding a person, showing a way. Two variants of the main page were created, both showing the idea of guiding with the help of the elements on the page - either guiding a ship to a spot or a top-view on a map. X marks the spot! Note, that I only created two concepts of the main page, one of which was accepted eventually, and another person finished the website.
